Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2025

Sugoi Shimeji (“we”, “our”, “the Extension”) respects your privacy. This page describes what data is collected when you visit sugoishimeji.github.io (“the Site”) or use the Sugoi Shimeji Chrome/Firefox extension, how that data is used, and the choices you have.

1. Data we do not collect

  • We do not ask for or store your name, e-mail, address, phone number, or payment details.
  • No keystrokes, browsing history, or form contents ever leave your device.
  • The Extension’s settings are saved locally using Chrome’s chrome.storage API and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Data collected automatically

Like most websites, the Site collects limited, pseudonymous information that your browser sends automatically:

  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Browser & device type
  • Referring URL and pages viewed
  • Date / time of visit

This information is used solely for aggregated traffic analytics, security (e.g. blocking abuse) and to comply with legal obligations.

3. Advertising & cookies

The Extension shows a small banner ad inside its popup. Ads are provided by AdMaven (“AdMaven”), an independent ad network that acts as an independent data controller. When an ad is displayed, AdMaven may:

  • Set or read cookies and local-storage identifiers for frequency-capping & analytics
  • Log your IP address, user-agent, and ad interaction events

AdMaven’s practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy.

Managing cookies / opting out

  • On the Site: the cookie banner shown to EU/UK visitors lets you accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • In the Extension: open the Settings panel and toggle “Hide ads” to disable the ad iframe completely. Your preference is stored locally and sent nowhere.

4. Legal bases for processing

  • Consent – for cookies and personalised ads where required (e.g. EU, UK).
  • Legitimate interest – to protect our service from abuse and measure anonymous traffic.

5. Your rights

If you are in the EEA/UK, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of “selling or sharing” personal data under the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise any of these rights, e-mail us at austinthemichaud@gmail.com. We may ask for additional information to verify your identity.

6. Data retention

Site server logs are kept for no more than 30 days unless we are investigating abuse. AdMaven retains ad logs as described in their policy.

7. Children’s privacy

Sugoi Shimeji is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. International transfers

We are based in Mexico; data may be processed on servers located in other countries with adequate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Third-party links

The Site may link to external pages (e.g. GitHub, Twitter). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Material changes will be announced on the Site and by bumping the “Last updated” date above.
